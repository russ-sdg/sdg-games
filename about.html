About Us

SDG Games is the game design business within SDG Strategy, LLC.

We love to play games and, as homeschoolers, some of their favorite games are educational. The idea for SDG Games grew out of our own desire to learn more about the Bible and what it teaches. There's nothing better than to learn life-changing truths while having fun with your family and friends!

We launched our first game, Journeys with Jesus in November 2020 so that we could better learn the geography of the Gospels. We have since launched 5 additional games and developed a new lower-cost version of Journeys with Jesus.

The names of our businesses reflect one of the battle cries of the Christian Reformation:
Soli Deo Gloria - to God alone be the glory.
