About Us

SDG Games is a division of SDG Strategy, LLC.

SDG Strategy is the consulting and coaching business of Russ McGuire. Through SDG Strategy, Russ helps business leaders make the most of their God-given talents and opportunities.

Russ has been a leader in technology industries for over 35 years. He has served as a strategy executive in large corporations and has founded or co-founded several technology startups. One of those startups was Christian Homeschool Network LLC where Russ helped his son and other homeschooled students launch CXfriends, the Facebook alternative for Christian families, which they ran from 2010 to 2015.

Russ has a heart for Christian families. He and his family love to play games and, as a homeschool family, some of their favorite games are educational. The idea for SDG Games grew out of Russ' own desire to learn more about the Bible and what it teaches. There's nothing better than to learn life-changing truths while having fun with your family and friends!

We launched our first game, Journeys with Jesus in November 2020 so that we could better learn the geography of the Gospels. We have since launched 5 additional games and developed a new lower-cost version of Journeys with Jesus.

The names of our businesses reflect one of the battle cries of the Christian Reformation:
Soli Deo Gloria - to God alone be the glory.
